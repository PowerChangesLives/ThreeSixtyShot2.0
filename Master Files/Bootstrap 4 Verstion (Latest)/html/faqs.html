A 360 photobooth is an innovative setup that captures images in a full 360-degree view. It provides an immersive experience where guests can stand in the center while the camera rotates around them, creating dynamic, captivating photos or videos.

Our 360 photobooth utilizes advanced technology and a motorized platform to rotate the camera around the subject. Guests stand on a designated spot, and the camera automatically captures their image from every angle as it completes a full rotation. The result is a stunning, interactive visual experience.

Our 360 photobooth is perfect for a wide range of events, including weddings, corporate parties, trade shows, birthdays, and product launches. Any occasion where you want to add a touch of excitement and entertainment, while also capturing memorable moments, is ideal for our photobooth.

We offer various customization options to tailor the experience to your event. You can choose custom backgrounds, overlays, logos, and even interactive elements to match your theme or branding. Additionally, guests can share their photos instantly on social media, further extending your event's reach.

Yes, we handle the complete setup and operation of the 360 photobooth. Our experienced team will ensure everything runs smoothly throughout your event, from assembly to assisting guests with using the photobooth. You can relax and enjoy the experience while we take care of the technical details.

ThreeSixtyShot is based in Morris Plains, NJ, but we provide services across the tri-state area, including New Jersey, New York, and Pennsylvania. Whether your event is in a bustling city or a serene countryside, we're ready to bring our 360 photobooth experience to you.
